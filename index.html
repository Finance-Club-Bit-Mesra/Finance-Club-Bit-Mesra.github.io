Who We Are

Finance Club

The Finance Club, BIT Mesra has been established with the aim to acquaint the students with economic and financial concepts enabling then to cope up with the complex financial world outside. The club provides the opportunity to gain various financial skills which will not only help them manage their personal finances well but also generate passive source of income. It gives the platform where finance enthusiasts can come together for all round holistic development in an atmosphere of friendly competition.

"Finance Club- BIT Mesra” a new student’s body society was started April 2021 for all UG and PG students not only in the BIT Mesra campus but also other off campus BIT Mesra across the country. The main objective of this club body is to acquaint the students with basic economic and financial concepts and terminology which in turn will hand over to them the required tools to cope up with the complex financial world outside and to manage their finances as well. This club shall guide students on how they can generate a passive source of income through the skills they possess through holistic development and learning how to make profitable investments with low risk requires time, patience and most importantly experience. The club wishes to incite students to take up the challenging task of knowing the world and its financial aspects, organize workshops, Quizzes, Webinar and hands on training in financial analytics and intelligence.
